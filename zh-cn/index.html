What is Dogecoin?

An open-source peer-to-peer digital currency, favoured by Shiba Inus worldwide.

At its heart, Dogecoin is the accidental crypto movement that makes people smile! It is also an opensource peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that utilises blockchain technology, a highly secure decentralised system of storing information as a public ledger that is maintained by a network of computers called nodes. More than this, though, is the ethos of Dogecoin, summarised in the Dogecoin Manifesto , and its amazing, vibrant community made up of friendly folks just like you! Learn more here

wallets

There are many ways you can get a wallet, and the best choice of wallet for you is dependent upon the purpose for which you intend to use your Dogecoin. There are two overarching categories of wallets: ‘custodial’ and ‘non-custodial’, depending on whether you are the sole holder of your wallet’s private key. The wallets listed on this website are non-custodial wallets, meaning that you possess and control the private keys.
